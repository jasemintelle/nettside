Jasemin Telle

Jasemin Telle, (b.1996, Bergen, NO) is a visual artist, currently working between Hasselt, Belgium and Bergen, Norway. Working mainly with textile, text and performance, her projects revolve around intimacy, landscapes and longing - with a strong connection to the domestic.
